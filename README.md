# Ring of Elysium Revival Initiative

> **Status:** Early stage · Seeking official partnership with Tencent · Community-driven technical research

## 🎯 Mission

This initiative is dedicated to the revival of **Ring of Elysium**, a battle royale game that was shut down in December 2023. Our goal is not piracy or unauthorized emulation — but **official, legal cooperation** with Tencent to bring the game back.

We are a group of DevOps engineers, reverse engineers, and passionate players who believe that:

- Great games deserve a second life
- Community-driven operations can be sustainable
- Official partnerships benefit both the developer and the players

## 📌 Current Focus

| Area | Status | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Official Contact** | 🔄 In Progress | We reached out to Tencent's BD department (Feb 2026) exploring licensing options |
| **Client Research** | 🧪 Active | Extracting and documenting game assets for preservation purposes (no server emulation yet) |
| **Community Building** | 🌱 Growing | Gathering former players, developers, and supporters |
| **Business Model** | 📝 Draft | Exploring sustainable models: subscription, cosmetic shop, community-funded |

## 🤝 Our Approach

We are pursuing **two parallel tracks**:

1. **Official Partnership Track**  
   - Contacting Tencent's business development and licensing departments  
   - Proposing a licensing or publishing agreement  
   - Willing to register a legal entity and comply with all requirements

2. **Preservation Track**  
   - Documenting the game's file formats and engine (QuickSilverX)  
   - Creating tools for archival purposes  
   - **No server emulation or copyright-infringing distribution**

If official licensing fails, this repository will remain as a **technical archive** of a game that deserved better.
